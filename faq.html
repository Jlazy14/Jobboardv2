How do I apply for Skillbridge?

Service members interested in Skillbridge should contact their respective Skillbridge Program Coordinator or Transition Assistance Program (TAP) office.


Who is eligible for Skillbridge?

Service members who are expected to be discharged or released from active duty service under honorable conditions may be eligible for Skillbridge.
